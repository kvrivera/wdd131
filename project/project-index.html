About The Author

Hello, my name is Kat and I am an avid (read: passionately excited) reader. I have been since I figured out how to piece letters together on a page to sound out words and tell stories that someone had neatly packed away for me in the storybooks of my school's library. Worlds without end! How could one walk past a book without wondering what great discoveries lay between the covers and beyond the weathering yellowed pages and blotted text! How can I not love reading? What is there not to love?

So here is my little corner of the internet! A place where I can talk about all of the books I have found a home in, and the books I have held hands with for a small walk, and even and especially the books that I have thrown across the room in disgust, contempt, or sheer excitement!!!!!!

If this sort of stuff doesn't excite you, then I fear you may find yourself exquisitely bored flipping through the figurative pages of the corner of my brain that houses my library of read-adventures. In which case I woudl recommend you skip along and find something more (read: something less, far far less) exciting to mull over.

I can't wait to go on this adventure with you, whoever you are reading this.
