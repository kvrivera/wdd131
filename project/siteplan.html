Name Explanation

As outlined in the purpose of this project (below), the goal in creating this website is to share my favourite books that have graced my shelves, both figuratively (through public library use), as well as literally (books I actually own). Through this project I hope to "share" my shelves, which is what inspired the name.

Site Purpose

This website provides a home for book recommendations and reviews for books that the site author (Kathleen) has read.

Scenarios

Someone new to a genre of literature is looking for recommendations to start their reading journey off.

Someone looking for books of a specific nature (eg: BIPOC characters, strong female lead) or tropes (eg: love triangle) that come highly recommended, or books to steer clear of.

A parent looking for storybooks for their child, which address specific scenarios or situations (eg: first day of school, death of a loved one, etc.).
